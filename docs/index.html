Why we built this

The popular tool LineIn was discontinued in 2017 and doesn't work on modern macOS. The alternatives cost $50-120. We thought that was absurd for such a simple utility, so we built a free one. Perfect for testing mics, monitoring yourself while recording podcasts, streaming, or just practicing your karaoke skills.
